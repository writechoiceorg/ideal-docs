Search Types

Individual

Searches for a specific individual.

Required Inputs

Individual

firstName, lastName, SSN

Certificates Available

  • Certificado de cumplimiento con la Junta de Retiro del Gobierno de Puerto Rico

Compliance Available

In compliance

  • No Payment Plan.
  • With a Payment plan and up to date with a payment plan (Tiene Plan de Pago Vigente).

Not in compliance

  • No Payment Plan (No Cumple).
  • With Payment plan and not up to date with a payment plan (No esta un cumplimiento con plan de pago).

Additional Data Received

Status

The status of the certificate creation.

SSN exist

Indicates if the individual social security number exists.

Payment plan

Indicates if the individual has a payment plan established with RETIRO.
